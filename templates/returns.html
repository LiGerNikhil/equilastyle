Return Policy

30-Day Return Window

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund or exchange.

Easy Returns

Simple online return process with prepaid shipping labels

Quick Processing

Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after return approval

Quality Guarantee

100% authentic products with quality assurance

Return Conditions

To be eligible for a return, items must meet the following conditions:

  • Items must be unused and in original condition
  • Original tags and labels must be intact
  • Original packaging must be included
  • Return request must be initiated within 30 days of delivery
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) required

Eligible Items

Returns Accepted

  • Footwear (all categories)
  • Apparel and clothing
  • Accessories (bags, belts, etc.)
  • Items with manufacturing defects
  • Wrong items delivered
  • Items damaged during shipping

Returns Not Accepted

  • Items worn or used
  • Items without original tags
  • Items with damaged packaging
  • Final sale items
  • Personalized items
  • Items returned after 30 days

Quality Checklist

Footwear

No signs of wear, clean soles, original box included

Apparel

No stains, tears, or alterations, tags attached

Accessories

No scratches or damage, original packaging intact

Exchange Policy

Size and Style Exchanges

Want a different size or color? We offer easy exchanges within 30 days of delivery:

Free Size Exchanges

Exchange Process

  1. Select "Exchange" instead of "Return" when initiating request
  2. Choose new size, color, or style from available options
  3. Pay any price difference (if applicable)
  4. Receive prepaid shipping label for return
  5. New item shipped once return is processed
  6. Exchange typically completed within 7-10 days

Exchange Conditions

  • Original item must meet return eligibility criteria
  • Exchanged item must be in stock
  • Only one exchange per order allowed
  • Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly

Damaged Items

Received Damaged Item?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, we're here to help:

Shipping Damage

Items damaged during transit

  • Report within 48 hours of delivery

Manufacturing Defects

Damage Claim Process

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the return process take?

Most returns are processed within 5-7 business days from when we receive your return.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

No, we provide prepaid shipping labels for all returns and exchanges.

Can I return items purchased on sale?

Yes, sale items can be returned unless marked as "Final Sale".

What if I lost my original packaging?

You can use secure alternative packaging, but original packaging is preferred.

How do I track my return?

You can track your return status in your account under "My Returns".
