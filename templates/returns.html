Return Policy

No Returns Accepted

EQUILA STYLE operates a no-return policy. All sales are final, and items cannot be returned for change of mind.

Returns are only considered for items that are damaged, defective, or incorrect at the time of delivery.

Final Sale

Items purchased are final unless a valid claim is approved.

Claims for Defects

Damage, defects, or wrong item delivery are evaluated on request.

Support Assistance

Contact our team promptly for claim support and resolution.

Claims Conditions

We review claims only when one of the following applies:

  • Item is damaged or defective on delivery
  • Wrong item was shipped to you
  • Proof of purchase and photos are provided
  • Claims are reported promptly after delivery
  • Items are not accepted for change of mind or fit issues

Claims Eligibility

Claims Accepted

  • Items damaged during shipping
  • Manufacturing defects on arrival
  • Incorrect item shipped
  • Items missing parts or accessories

Returns Not Accepted

  • Change of mind or fit issues
  • Items worn, used, or altered
  • Items without original tags
  • Final sale items
  • Personalized or customized products
  • All other standard returns

Claims Checklist

Footwear

Report only if received damaged, defective, or incorrect

Apparel

Claims approved only for defects or wrong items

Accessories

Only damaged or incorrect accessories are eligible for claim review

Claim Process

Our claims process is designed for damaged, defective, or incorrect deliveries only.

1

Report Issue

Contact us immediately with order details and photos of the item.

2

Claim Review

We review your claim and confirm eligibility for return or replacement.

3

Return Approval

Approved claims receive instructions for return shipping, if required.

4

Package Items

Follow the claim instructions and pack the item securely for transit.

5

Inspect Item

We inspect the returned item to verify the claim.

6

Resolution

Approved claims receive a refund, replacement, or store credit.

Exchange Policy

No Exchanges

We do not offer exchanges for size, color, or style changes. All sales are final unless an item is damaged, defective, or incorrect.

No Size Exchanges

Size exchanges are not available under this policy.

No Style Changes

Requests for a different color or style are not accepted.

Claims Only

Replacement is only possible for approved defect or wrong-item claims.

Policy Conditions

  • No exchanges for change of mind
  • Only valid claims may be reviewed for replacement
  • Replacement depends on item availability and claim approval
  • Price differences are not applicable

Step 1: Prepare Claim Items

Pack only the items approved for return under your claim.

Damaged Items

Received Damaged Item?

If you receive a damaged or defective item, we're here to help:

Shipping Damage

Items damaged during transit

  • Report within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of damage and packaging
  • Free replacement or full refund
  • No return shipping required

Manufacturing Defects

Items with quality or manufacturing issues

  • Report within 30 days of delivery
  • Free replacement or repair
  • Return shipping provided
  • Priority processing

Wrong Item

Received different item than ordered

  • Report immediately upon receipt
  • Correct item shipped immediately
  • Return pickup arranged at no cost
  • Expedited shipping for replacement

Damage Claim Process

  1. Document the issue with clear photos
  2. Contact customer support immediately
  3. Provide order number and damage details
  4. Choose replacement or refund option
  5. Receive resolution within 48 hours

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the return process take?

Most returns are processed within 5-7 business days from when we receive your return.

Do I have to pay for return shipping?

Return shipping is provided only for approved damage, defect, or wrong-item claims. General returns are not accepted.

Can I return items purchased on sale?

No. Sale items are final sale unless they are received damaged, defective, or incorrect.

What if I lost my original packaging?

For approved claim returns, use secure alternative packaging and follow the instructions provided.

How do I track my return?

You can track your return status in your account under "My Returns".
