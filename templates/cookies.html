What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better shopping experience by:

Remembering Your Preferences

Keeping track of your language, currency, and display preferences

Shopping Cart Functionality

Maintaining your cart items as you browse our collections

Personalized Experience

Showing you products and recommendations tailored to your interests

Security

Protecting your account and preventing fraudulent activities

Technical Information

Cookies contain unique identifiers that allow our website to recognize your browser on subsequent visits. They don't store personal information like your name or address, but they help us remember your preferences and activities.

How We Use Cookies

At EQUILA STYLE, we use cookies to enhance your premium shopping experience:

Authentication & Security

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Protect your session from unauthorized access
  • Verify your identity during checkout

Personalization

  • Measure marketing campaign effectiveness
  • Provide personalized offers and promotions

Types of Cookies

We use different types of cookies, each serving a specific purpose:

Essential Cookies

Required

These cookies are necessary for our website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation, access to secure areas, and shopping cart management.

Examples:
  • Session cookies for login authentication
  • Shopping cart persistence
  • Security tokens

Preference Cookies

Optional

These cookies remember your choices and preferences to provide you with a personalized experience.

Examples:
  • Language and currency settings
  • Display preferences
  • Remembered login information

Analytics Cookies

Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

Examples:
  • Google Analytics cookies
  • Heat mapping and user behavior tracking
  • Performance monitoring

Marketing Cookies

Optional

These cookies are used to deliver advertisements that are relevant to you and your interests.

Examples:
  • Facebook Pixel
  • Google Ads conversion tracking
  • Retargeting cookies

Your Control

You can control third-party cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent banner. Disabling these cookies may affect some website features.

Managing Your Preferences

You have several options to manage your cookie preferences:

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our site, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies for the best experience
  • Customize which cookie categories you allow
  • Reject non-essential cookies

You can change your preferences at any time using the cookie settings widget.

Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your web browser:

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data

Edge

Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Mobile Devices

Mobile browsers have similar cookie management options:

  • iOS Safari: Settings → Safari → Block Cookies
  • Android Chrome: Settings → Site settings → Cookies
  • Other browsers: Check browser-specific settings

Important Note

Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your shopping experience. Some features like your shopping cart, personalized recommendations, and quick checkout may not work properly without cookies.

Cookie Lifetime

Different cookies have different lifespans:

Session Cookies

Duration: Deleted when you close your browser

Purpose: Temporary data like shopping cart contents

Persistent Cookies

Duration: 30 days to 2 years, depending on purpose

Purpose: Remembering preferences and login status

Refresh Cookies

Duration: Renewed each time you visit

Purpose: Long-term preferences and analytics

Automatic Cleanup

Our system automatically removes expired cookies to maintain optimal performance. You can also manually clear cookies through your browser settings.
