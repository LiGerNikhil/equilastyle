Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you shop with EQUILA STYLE, we collect information to provide you with the best shopping experience:

  • Name and contact details: Email, phone number, shipping address
  • Account information: Username, password (encrypted), profile preferences
  • Payment information: Payment method details (processed securely by third-party payment processors)
  • Shopping preferences: Size preferences, favorite brands, wishlist items

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and browser data: Browser type, operating system, IP address
  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns
  • Location data: Approximate geographic location for regional pricing and shipping

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to enhance your shopping experience and provide our services:

  • Process orders: Fulfill purchases, arrange shipping, send order confirmations
  • Personalize experience: Show relevant products, remember preferences
  • Communications: Send order updates, newsletters (with consent)
  • Improve services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance our platform
  • Security: Prevent fraud, ensure secure transactions
  • Legal compliance: Meet regulatory requirements

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your information only when necessary for our services:

Shipping Partners

Delivery services need your address to fulfill orders

Payment Processors

Secure payment processing (we don't store full card details)

Analytics Services

Anonymous usage data to improve our website

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted
  • Secure Storage: Personal data stored on secure servers
  • Access Control: Limited employee access to customer data
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates
  • PCI Compliance: Payment Card Industry standards for payment processing

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality (shopping cart, checkout)

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our site

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and other privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request removal of your personal data
  • Portability: Transfer your data to another service
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your information
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

International Data Transfers

EQUILA STYLE operates globally and may transfer data internationally. We ensure:

  • Adequate protection levels in recipient countries
  • Standard contractual clauses with international partners
  • Compliance with international data transfer regulations
