Delivery Instructions

Ensure smooth delivery by following these guidelines:

Address Details

  • Provide complete and accurate address
  • Include landmark for easy location
  • Add contact number for delivery coordination
  • Mention nearest famous location if needed

Availability

  • Ensure someone is available to receive delivery
  • Provide alternate contact number
  • Specify preferred delivery time if possible
  • Inform security/reception in apartment buildings

Security

  • Check ID of delivery personnel
  • Verify package condition before accepting
  • Don't accept damaged packages
  • Report issues immediately

Special Delivery Instructions

You can add special delivery instructions during checkout:

  • "Leave with building security"
  • "Call before delivery"
  • "Deliver after 6 PM"
  • "Gate code: #1234"

Shipping Issues

If you encounter any shipping issues, we're here to help:

Wrong Address

Contact us immediately if you provided an incorrect address. We'll try to redirect if the package hasn't been dispatched.

Package Not Received

If tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package, check with neighbors and building security within 24 hours.

Damaged Package

Reject damaged packages or report within 48 hours. We'll arrange for replacement or refund.

Delayed Delivery

Check tracking for updates. Contact us if delivery is delayed beyond estimated time.

Issue Resolution Process

  1. Contact customer support with order details
  2. Provide description and photos if applicable
  3. Our team investigates within 24 hours
  4. Resolution offered within 48 hours
  5. Follow-up until issue is resolved

Common Questions

Can I change my shipping address after order?

Address changes are possible within 2 hours of order placement. Contact us immediately.

Do you ship on weekends?

We process orders on business days. Delivery attempts may happen on Saturdays depending on courier.

What if I'm not available for delivery?

The courier will attempt delivery twice. After that, the package will be returned to our warehouse.
